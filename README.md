# Race Pace Calculator

## Specification Deliverable
### Elevator Pitch
Those in the running community seek to continually improve and acheive personal goals, running faster and faster in races. As with other aspects of life, careful planning is key to success in a race. Runners often pick a goal time and calculate the paces they need to hit to acheive that time. Often they engage in mental math or use quickly scribbled calculations. This web service seeks to aid them by providing a place where they can calculate their ideal race paces and track their goals and progress all in one website.
